About the Game

We kept it simple - just pure bubble shooting without all the extra stuff that clutters up other games.
Players aim, shoot, and clear bubbles through increasingly challenging layouts.
As you get further, the levels get trickier with weird angles and stacked bubbles, so you really gotta plan your shots.
It’s a balance of relaxation and focus, making each level both enjoyable and rewarding.

Gameplay Mechanics

  • Aim and Shoot

    Players launch bubbles toward matching colors. Groups of three or more pop instantly, clearing space and triggering potential chain reactions.

  • Smart Positioning

    Sometimes you can't just shoot straight - you'll need to bank shots off the walls to hit those tricky spots or set up bigger combos.

Key Features

  • Classic bubble shooter gameplay

  • Gradual difficulty progression

  • Clean and readable interface

  • Satisfying pop and chain reactions

  • Short, replayable levels

Level Progression

Levels begin with simple formations that allow players to learn the mechanics. Over time, layouts become denser and more layered, requiring precise shots and better planning. Each level's a bit harder than the one before, but not in a way that'll make you want to throw your phone.
